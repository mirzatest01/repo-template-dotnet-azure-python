# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and professional environment
for everyone participating in this organizationâ€™s repositories and activities.

## Expected Behavior
We expect all contributors to:
- Be respectful, considerate, and collaborative
- Provide constructive feedback
- Assume positive intent
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences
- Follow secure and ethical engineering practices

## Unacceptable Behavior
Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment, discrimination, or offensive language
- Personal attacks or intimidation
- Publishing private or confidential information
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or development workflows
- Abuse of AI tools to bypass reviews, security, or compliance controls

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All repositories within this organization
- Issues, pull requests, discussions, and code reviews
- Any official communication channels related to the organization

## Enforcement
Project maintainers and organization administrators are responsible for enforcing
this Code of Conduct. Violations may result in actions including:
- Warning or request for corrective action
- Temporary or permanent removal of access
- Escalation to organizational leadership or HR (where applicable)

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it by:
- Contacting the repository maintainers, or
- Emailing: security@your-org.com or conduct@your-org.com

All reports will be reviewed confidentially and handled promptly.
